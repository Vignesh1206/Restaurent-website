Welcome to Ratatoullie

Ratatoullie is a fine dining restaurant located in the heart of Puducheery. The restaurant overlooks 337, Mission St, MG Road Area,Puducherry.Located here since 1998, it underwent a full-scale renovation and redesign in the summer of 2017. The restaurant has been owned by Chef Daniel Humm since 2011 and during that time it has evolved considerably, both in food and in experience.Today, the restaurant offers an eight to ten course menu in the main dining room, but guests can also visit the bar for a more abbreviated tasting menu, some light snacks, or simply for a cocktail or glass of wine.
